WELCOME TO OUR B&B

YOUR BEST B&B


A cozy house in the heart of Amsterdam on Prinsengracht, which considered to be a central and sophisticated location.


Amsjoy offers room with free WiFi, conveniently located within a walking distance from Anne Frank House and Leidseplein. The property is located within a 9-minute walk from Flower Market, and a 9-minute walk from Royal Palace Amsterdam on Dam Square. The property is set in Amsterdam City Center district, close to Rembrandtplein.

The units in the bed and breakfast are equipped with a kettle. Guest rooms will provide guests with a microwave. In this apartment, you have shared the hallway with other our guests and doors to the room are without the lock.

Museum of Bags and Purses Hendrikje is 0.7 mi from Amsjoy, while Rijksmuseum is 0.7 mi away. Schiphol Airport is 6.8 mi from the property.
